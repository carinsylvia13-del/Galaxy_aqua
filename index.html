Founder’s note

Built on a simple belief: good water guidance should feel clear.

“Every customer deserves a solution that fits their water, their space and the way they actually live or work.”

David Santhamohan is the sole founder of Galaxy Aqua Solutions. He started the organisation in 2014 with a focused purpose: to make dependable water-treatment products and practical guidance easier to access.

Under his leadership, Galaxy Aqua Solutions brings domestic, commercial and component solutions into one considered portfolio. The site is designed to help visitors understand the options, narrow the right category and connect for a recommendation suited to their needs.
